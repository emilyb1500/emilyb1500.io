Coming from our own personal research at SCAA, we observed 100 cats over a 5 month period in their homes and studied their response times when it came to feeding time. We discovered that 100% of cats arrived at their food bowls within 5 minutes of feeding time. However when cats were called outside of their main feeding time, only 10% responded to our calls. This not only suggests that cats can understand time related routines, but that they selectively choose to acknowledge us based on how they feel.
